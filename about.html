About Us

Red Fox Boutique started its journey as an idea to create a sustainable and ethical brand focusing on size inclusive ranges to stock in our store.

Located in Fox Hollows, NSW 2000 we have finally opened our first physical store since starting our journey online in 2021. We stock many brands and items inclusive in most sizes to provide varitey to all patriates.
